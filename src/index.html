How it works

              
    
                
  1. Set your game folder in Settings — it'll try to auto-detect from Steam.
    2. 
                
  2. Drop mod folders or .zip files anywhere in the app to import them into the library.
    3. 
                
  3. Toggle mods on or off in My Mods.
    4. 
                
  4. Click Apply Mods or Apply & Play to deploy enabled mods and optionally launch the game.
    5.

Notes

              
    
                
  • Toggling a mod only saves the preference — files don't move until you Apply.
    • 
                
  • Mods are stored in the app's local library and copied to the game folder on Apply.
    • 
                
  • Removing a mod deletes it from the library and removes its files on next Apply.
